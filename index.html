Boredom + Free time = Business Idea?

Just a brief backstory, my first business idea was nowhere near Can'tCook— well, the name was close. I was thinking of making artisan keycaps, Can'tType (see the resemblance?), because of my obsession with mechanical keyboards. But the pandemic is rough and I had problems with suppliers. Boredom struck and I tried cooking new recipes to relieve stress, then the rest is history.
